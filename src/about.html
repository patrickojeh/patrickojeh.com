About


I'm a product engineer based in Lagos, Nigeria, where I've worked professionally for most of my career (outside of remote roles). During this time, I amassed valuable experience working across diverse environments, including startups, agencies, and large companies.

By October 2017, I joined a development agency as a designer/developer. During this time, I contributed to various projects, including an engineering platform for Well planning & design for Petrocompletions, the Afrinvestor stock trading and investment mobile application, and updates to the in-house software, Prowork. My focus was on product design and UI engineering.

Following a fulfilling four-year stint at Mobnia, I had the opportunity to contract as a product engineer for Paystack. During this engagement, I collaborated with a highly skilled team to develop a digital archive for the in-house Design Studio.

As of June 2023, I joined INITS, a leading software development and technology consulting company, where I work as a senior frontend engineer.

For a more detailed account of my professional background, request my resume or connect with me on LinkedIn.
